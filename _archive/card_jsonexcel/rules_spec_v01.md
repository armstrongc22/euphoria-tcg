# Euphoria TCG Rules Spec v0.1

## Match Setup
- Deck size: 30 cards
- Starting hand: 5 cards
- Starting Spirit: 1
- Spirit gained per turn: +1 at the start of each turn
- Max Spirit: none
- Lives: 3
- Direct attacks: only if the opponent controls no Warriors
- Player 1 draws on the first turn
- Spirit is gained before drawing

## Turn Structure

### Start Phase
- Gain 1 Spirit.
- Draw 1 card.
- Refresh Warriors.
- Resolve delayed effects.

### Main Phase
The active player may:
- Summon Warriors
- Play Items
- Equip Weapons

Attack cards are not freely played in Main Phase. They are prompted when a Warrior attacks another Warrior.

### Battle Phase
Once Battle Phase begins:
- Main Phase is locked.
- Items cannot be played.
- Weapons cannot be equipped.
- Warriors may attack.
- When a Warrior attacks another Warrior, the player may use compatible Attack cards from hand.
- Multiple compatible Attack cards may be used on the same attack if the player has enough Spirit.
- Each Attack card resolves fully before the next Attack card is chosen.
- After Attack card choices are finished, combat damage resolves simultaneously.
- No Attack cards are offered on direct attacks.

### End Phase
- Expire temporary effects.
- Pass turn.

## Combat Rules
- No attacks are allowed on the first turn of the game.
- Warriors can attack the turn they are summoned after the first turn.
- Each Warrior may attack once per turn unless a card effect says otherwise.
- Multiple Warriors can attack in one turn.
- Combat damage is simultaneous.
- Direct attacks are allowed only if the opponent controls no Warriors.
- Only one direct attack is allowed per turn.

## Card Timing
- Items: Main Phase only.
- Weapons: Main Phase only.
- Attack cards: Battle Phase only, when one of your Warriors attacks another Warrior.
- No cards can be activated during the opponent's turn.
- No quick-play cards.
- No chain stack.
- Sequential effect stacking is allowed during your own turn if you have enough Spirit.

## Weapons
- One Weapon per Warrior.
- Weapons cannot be replaced.
- Weapons cannot be moved.
- If the equipped Warrior is destroyed, the Weapon goes to the Out deck.

## Out Deck
The Out deck is the discard/destroyed zone.

Cards that go to the Out deck:
- Used Items
- Used Attack cards
- Destroyed Warriors
- Destroyed Weapons

## Attack Card Compatibility
An Attack card is compatible if:
1. The player has enough Spirit.
2. The Attack card faction matches the attacking Warrior's faction, or the Attack card is Neutral.
3. The Attack card target requirements can be met.
4. No active effect prevents its use.

## No Chains
Stacking effects means a player may play/use multiple cards in sequence during their own turn, with each card fully resolving before the next one.

It does not mean:
- Responding to an opponent from hand.
- Countering responses.
- Activating cards during the opponent's turn.
